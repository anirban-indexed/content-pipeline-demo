## About Smart Fog

Smart Fog manufactures precision industrial humidification systems used across manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, data centers, printing, cold storage, food processing, aerospace, defense, healthcare, and electronics facilities.

**Core technology:** Compressed air and water are mixed through a proprietary nozzle to produce an equal-sized droplet grid. Each droplet is the same size and slightly charged to prevent re-aggregation. The droplets self-evaporate before reaching any surface, enabling precise, non-wetting humidity control up to 99% RH with plus or minus 1-2% precision.

**Key differentiators:**
- Non-wetting: humidifies without wetting surfaces, equipment, racks, ducts, or products under proper system design
- Precision: maintains humidity up to 99% RH with plus or minus 1-2% precision and minimal fluctuation
- 100% water efficient: every drop evaporates into the air
- No moving parts in the humidification process
- Low maintenance: no constant nozzle cleaning; maintenance intervals extend up to every two years
- Easy installation: no certified technician required
- Complete solution: not a component kit; Smart Fog designs and delivers the full engineered system
- Made in the USA with high-quality components
- 24/7 set-and-forget continuous industrial operation

**What Smart Fog is not:**
- Not steam, not traditional misting, not spray-based
- Not a dehumidification system
- Not a disinfectant -- no sterility or pathogen elimination claims
- Does not cause condensation, rust, mold, or surface wetting under proper system design

**Non-wetting caveat:** "Non-wetting" applies to surfaces under proper system design. Because Smart Fog systems use water and operate with force, direct exposure to the fog stream -- such as placing a hand directly into it -- will wet the hand. This caveat must be included whenever strong non-wetting claims are made.
